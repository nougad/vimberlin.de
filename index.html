Vimberlin is gathering for Vim-enthusiasts in Berlin. Our goal is to bring new and trained people using Vim together to talk about their practices, tool, and their habits working with Vim. If you want to give a talk (either a short 5 minute presentation or a longer one), please post your proposal on our mailing list or ping us on twitter.
